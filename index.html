Game Developer

Board Game Designer

I'm a third-year Game Development student at Malmö University, currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in game development. My primary development experience lies in object oriented programming in C# and creating games in Unity, involving everything from game design to programming gameplay systems. I also have a minor knowledge of C++ and Java, allowing me to adapt to a variety of development environments.

My specialization is in game AI programming, ranging from fundamental finite state machines and planners to advanced behavior trees. I’m passionate about crafting intelligent, believable NPCs that enhance player immersion and bring game worlds to life, an area of programming in which I aim to continue growing and deepening my expertise.

In my free time, I’m also a board game designer, with my first board game set to be published soon through a partner publisher. Whether it's video games or tabletop experiences, games have always been a central part of my life. That passion fuels my drive to work in a field where creativity, technical skill, and enjoyment go hand-in-hand.

I'm currently seeking opportunities to join a passionate development team. Find me interesting? Don’t hesitate to reach out!

Champion's Dispute (Board Game)

Champion's Dispute is a free for all battle royal game between 3-6 players. The objective is to emerge as the sole surviving player by defeating other players in combat. There are six unique champions (classes) that you will be able to choose from, each having their own strengths and weaknesses to play around.

Champion's Dispute is my largest project to date. I personally handled everything from the art and game design to the graphic design and component creation myself.
